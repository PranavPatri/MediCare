FAQs

What is SkinHealth?
SkinHealth is a healthcare portal that connects users with hospitals and healthcare providers. It features a machine learning model for quick skin disease assessment, facilitating immediate and informed healthcare decisions. In addition, SkinHealth provides information on skin health, medication guidance, and privacy policy. SkinHealth is designed to be user-centric, accessible, and efficient for individuals seeking skincare solutions.
